Pizza

Description

Pizza is a food everyone love. No matter the age it is something that no one is gonna turn down

This is a recipe for a delicious homemade pizza. Follow the directions and you will fall in love with the final result

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. Spread sauce on crust
  2. Sprinkle Cheese
  3. Add Pepperoni
  4. Bake 200c for 12-15 minutes or until curst is golden brown
  5. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving
